Couscous

Couscous: A Staple of Moroccan Cuisine

Discover the Simplicity and Versatility of Couscous

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups couscous
  • 2 cups water or chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or butter

Steps

  1. Boil Liquid: In a pot, bring water or chicken broth and olive oil (or butter) to a boil.
  2. Prepare Couscous: Place couscous in a heatproof bowl. Pour the boiling liquid over the couscous, cover with a lid or foil, and let it sit for about 5 minutes.
  3. Fluff and Serve: Use a fork to fluff the couscous, breaking up any clumps. Serve it as a side dish or as a base for flavorful tagines or stews.
