Harira: Taste the Traditional Moroccan Comfort

"A Hearty, Flavorful Soup Perfect for Every Occasion"

Ingredients

  • Certainly, here are the ingredients for Harira soup presented as bullet points: 1 cup dried chickpeas (garbanzo beans), soaked overnight
  • 1/2 cup lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped cilantro (coriander)
  • 1 cup chicken broth or water
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges (for serving)
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Prepare the Legumes: Drain and rinse the soaked chickpeas. In a large pot, combine the chickpeas, lentils, and rice. Cover with water (about 8 cups), bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for about 45 minutes or until the legumes are tender.
  2. Sauté Aromatics: In a separate pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onions, celery, tomatoes, cilantro, and parsley. Sauté until the onions are translucent.
  3. Spice Infusion: Add ground ginger, turmeric, cinnamon, paprika, black pepper, and salt to the sautéed mixture. Cook for a few minutes until the spices release their aroma.
  4. Combine and Simmer: Transfer the sautéed mixture to the pot with the cooked legumes. Stir well and let the soup simmer for another 20-30 minutes, allowing flavors to meld.
  5. Adjust Consistency: If the soup is too thick, add more water to reach your desired consistency. Adjust seasoning as needed.
  6. Serve: Ladle the hot Harira soup into bowls. Serve with a wedge of lemon on the side for squeezing over the soup before enjoying.
  7. Optional: For added texture, cook a handful of vermicelli or broken-up angel hair pasta separately and add it to the soup before serving.
