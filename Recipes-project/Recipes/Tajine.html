Tajine

An easy Tajine recipe

"Discover the Authenticity of Tajine: A Moroccan Culinary Delight, Bursting with Exotic Flavors and Aromas."

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs (or any preferred chicken parts)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 preserved lemon (rinsed, pulp removed, and thinly sliced)
  • 1 cup chicken broth or water
  • Handful of green olives
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Prepare the Chicken: Season the chicken thighs with salt, pepper, ground ginger, ground cumin, paprika, and ground cinnamon. Rub the seasoning well into the chicken.
  2. Sauté Onion and Garlic: In a tajine or a deep skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the finely chopped onion and sauté until softened. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  3. Brown the Chicken: Push the onion and garlic to the sides of the tajine and add the seasoned chicken thighs. Brown them on both sides for about 5 minutes per side.
  4. Add Liquid and Simmer: Pour in the chicken broth or water. Arrange the slices of preserved lemon over the chicken. Bring it to a simmer, cover the tajine, and let it cook for 25-30 minutes or until the chicken is tender and fully cooked.
  5. Add Olives and Serve: Add the green olives to the tajine during the last 10 minutes of cooking to infuse their flavor into the dish. Check the seasoning and adjust if necessary.
  6. Garnish and Serve: Sprinkle chopped fresh cilantro or parsley over the tajine before serving. Serve the chicken tajine hot with couscous or crusty bread to soak up the delicious sauce.
